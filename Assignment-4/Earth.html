EARTH

  1. Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only known planet to support life.

  2. About 71% of Earth’s surface is covered by water, making it unique in the solar system.

  3. The planet has a layered structure consisting of the crust, mantle, and core.

  4. Earth’s atmosphere is composed primarily of nitrogen (78%) and oxygen (21%).

  5. The planet rotates on its axis once every 24 hours, giving us day and night.

  6. Earth's axial tilt of 23.5 degrees is responsible for the changing seasons.

  7. The planet has one natural satellite, the Moon, which stabilizes Earth’s tilt and affects ocean tides.

  8. Earth is approximately 4.54 billion years old, based on geological evidence.

  9. The planet’s magnetic field protects it from harmful solar radiation.

  10. Human activities on Earth have significantly impacted its climate, ecosystems, and biodiversity.
